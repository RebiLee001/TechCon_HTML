Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small developer meetup in San Francisco. Over the years, it has grown into a global platform for innovation and collaboration, welcoming thousands of tech enthusiasts and professionals.

Our Mission

At TechCon, our mission is to connect innovators and creators from all corners of the tech world. We strive to promote open knowledge sharing, inspire future leaders, and drive meaningful conversations around technology for good.

Notable Past Speakers

  • Love Alalibo – Pioneering visionary in computing theory. Spoke on AI ethics and early algorithms.
  • Edward Akpa – Computing legend who discussed machine learning and theoretical computer science.
  • Grace Hopper – Advocate for women in STEM and inventor of the first compiler. Talked on innovation in programming.
